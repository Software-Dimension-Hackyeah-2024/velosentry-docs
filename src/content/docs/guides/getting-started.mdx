---

VeloSentry is a REST API that gives access to a set of tools for estimating the safety of a path from the perspective of a cyclist.
Its aim is to provide apps and other services with data necessary to make traveling with a bike safer, whether in the city or in rural areas.

## Pricing

Our API is free to use in its alpha stage, we may choose to put it under a pricing model in the future.
